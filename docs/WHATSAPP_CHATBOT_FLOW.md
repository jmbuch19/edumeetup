```
You can request a direct video meeting with a
```

```
Campus Fairs are official events where multiple universities

Check upcoming fairs after logging in:
👉 https://edumeetup.com/login
```

### S5 — Documents Needed
```
To get started on EdUmeetup, you need very little! 📄

For your *profile* (required):

• City and PIN code

For *applying to universities* (depends on the university):

• Passport copy
• English test score (IELTS / TOEFL / PTE / Duolingo)
• Statement of Purpose (SOP)
• Letters of Recommendation (LOR)

EdUmeetup helps you understand what each university needs 
specific to your profile. Register to get started:
👉 https://edumeetup.com/student/register
```

- [ ] Add fallback keyword triggers: `team`, `human`, `support`, `help`
